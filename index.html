About Me

Hi, I'm Rohan Bait. I am a Front-End Developer with a passion for creating engaging and user-friendly web experiences. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy turning ideas into beautiful and functional websites.

My journey in web development began several years ago when I discovered my fascination with the intersection of design and technology. Since then, I have continuously honed my skills and stayed up-to-date with the latest industry trends and best practices.

In addition to my technical skills, I bring creativity and attention to detail to every project. I believe that effective communication and collaboration are essential for delivering successful outcomes. I enjoy working in dynamic teams where I can contribute my expertise and learn from others.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new hiking trails, experimenting with photography, or immersing myself in a good book. I find inspiration in the world around me and strive to incorporate it into my work.
