About Me

Hi, I'm Rohan Bait. I am a Cloud & DevOps Enthusiast and a 2024 graduate. I started my professional journey as a Desktop Support Engineer, where I gained hands-on experience in troubleshooting, IT support, and system management.

Over time, my passion for automation, cloud computing, and scalable infrastructure led me to transition into the Cloud & DevOps space. I am actively working with AWS, Terraform, Docker, ECS, ECR, EC2, Nginx, Linux, Networking, and Scripting to design and deploy robust, production-grade systems.

I am focused on building automated, efficient, and maintainable cloud infrastructures. I enjoy solving complex problems, optimizing workflows, and bridging the gap between development and operations through CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure-as-code.

Outside work, I enjoy exploring cloud technologies, improving my Linux and scripting skills, and experimenting with new tools to stay ahead in the rapidly evolving DevOps ecosystem.
